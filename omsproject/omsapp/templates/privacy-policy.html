Privacy Policy

Farmer Harvest is a unified aggregator marketplace designed to empower farmer collectives by providing them with a seamless platform to sell their produce directly to a wide range of buyers. Our platform connects Farmer Producer Companies (FPCs), Cooperatives, and Producer Groups—each legally registered in India—with consumers, businesses, and institutional buyers, ensuring a fair and transparent marketplace for agricultural trade.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, contact details, billing and shipping addresses, and payment information.
  • Account Information: Username, password, and transaction history.
  • Usage Data: IP addresses, device details, browsing patterns, and interactions with our platform.
  • Business Information: For seller accounts, we may collect legal registration details, business addresses, and tax-related information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Facilitate transactions between buyers and sellers.
  • Improve and personalize user experience.
  • Process payments securely.
  • Provide customer support and resolve disputes.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share data with:

  • Sellers: To facilitate order fulfillment and communication.
  • Service Providers: Payment gateways, logistics partners, and third-party integrations for operational purposes.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law, regulation, or legal process.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we encourage users to take necessary precautions.

5. User Rights and Choices

Users have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete their personal information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Request information on data usage and processing.

6. Data Storage & Compliance

All user data is stored and processed in compliance with The Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 and other relevant Indian data privacy laws.

Data is securely stored on India-based servers to ensure compliance with national regulations.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience, analyze website traffic, and improve services. Users can manage cookie preferences through browser settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

Farmer Harvest reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. We encourage users to review this page periodically for any changes.
