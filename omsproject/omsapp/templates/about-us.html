About Farmer Harvest

Farmer Harvest is a unified aggregator marketplace designed to empower farmer collectives by providing them with a seamless platform to sell their produce directly to a wide range of buyers. Our platform connects Farmer Producer Companies (FPCs), Cooperatives, and Producer Groups—each legally registered in India—with consumers, businesses, and institutional buyers, ensuring a fair and transparent marketplace for agricultural trade.

A Marketplace for Farmer Collectives

Unlike conventional marketplaces, Farmer Harvest exclusively onboards legally registered farmer collectives, ensuring authenticity, traceability, and quality assurance. By eliminating multiple middle layer supply chain enablers, we help farmers receive better value for their produce while offering consumers direct access to fresh, high-quality agricultural goods.

Catering to Diverse Buyer Needs

Farmer Harvest serves a diverse customer base, including:

  • Retail consumers looking for fresh and ethically sourced produce.
  • Businesses such as grocery stores, wholesalers, and food processors.
  • Institutional buyers including schools, hostels, and religious institutions needing bulk fresh produce.
  • Buyers beyond the local geography, helping farmer collectives reach wider markets.

Dual Sales Channels: Hyperlocal & Extended Reach

Farmer Harvest operates on both hyperlocal and extended reach models:

  • Sales: Farmer collectives serve local consumers and institutions with quick deliveries. Bulk orders from schools and religious places support steady demand.
  • Extended Reach Sales: The platform enables collectives to reach buyers beyond their geographic limits, expanding market access.

Powered by Advanced Technology

Farmer Harvest is built on cutting-edge technology to streamline operations for both farmers and buyers. The platform integrates easily with third-party services, offering tools for inventory management, order tracking, and logistics support—enabling professional and scalable agri-commerce.

Developed by Agro Visionary Technologies Pvt. Ltd.

Farmer Harvest is a proprietary platform developed and owned by Agro Visionary Technologies Private Limited, reflecting our commitment to transforming agricultural trade through innovation and inclusivity.

Join us in revolutionizing how farmer collectives connect with buyers—locally and nationally—building a sustainable and prosperous future for Indian agriculture.
