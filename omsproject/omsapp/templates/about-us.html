About Farmer Harvest

Farmer Harvest is a unified aggregator marketplace designed to empower farmer collectives by providing them with a seamless platform to sell their produce directly to a wide range of buyers. Our platform connects Farmer Producer Companies (FPCs), Cooperatives, and Producer Groups—each legally registered in India—with consumers, businesses, and institutional buyers, ensuring a fair and transparent marketplace for agricultural trade.

A Marketplace for Farmer Collectives/h3>

Unlike conventional marketplaces, Farmer Harvest exclusively onboards legally registered farmer collectives, ensuring authenticity, traceability, and quality assurance. By eliminating multiple middle layer supply chain enablers, we help farmers receive better value for their produce while offering consumers direct access to fresh, high-quality agricultural goods.

Catering to Diverse Buyer Needs

Farmer Harvest serves a diverse customer base, including:

  • Retail consumerslooking for fresh and ethically sourced produce.
  • Businessessuch as grocery stores, wholesalers, and food processors.
  • Institutional buyersincluding schools, residential hostels, and religious institutions that require bulk agricultural supplies for preparing fresh meals.
  • Buyers beyond the local geographyenabling farmer collectives to expand their reach to customers outside their immediate operational area.

Dual Sales Channels: Hyperlocal & Extended Reach

Farmer Harvest operates on both hyperlocal and extended reach sales models:

  • Sales: Farmer collectives directly serve local consumers and institutions within their respective regions, ensuring quick and efficient delivery. Bulk procurement by institutions such as schools and religious establishments ensures steady demand for farmers' produce.
  • Extended Reach Sales:Beyond local boundaries, Farmer Harvest enables farmer collectives to connect with buyers across different regions, expanding their market reach without being restricted to their immediate geography.

Powered by Advanced Technology

Farmer Harvest is built on cutting-edge technology to streamline operations for both farmers and buyers. The platform is designed for easy integration with third-party services, offering farmer collectives comprehensive business management solutions, from inventory tracking to logistics support.
